ProxemX: VR Theatre

ProxemX is a virtual reality environment for remote theatrical rehearsal and production. Collaborative virtual environments afford the great benefit of facilitating co-presence, a sense of ‘being together’, and can provide practical tools to enhance theatre rehearsal and production. The impact of the COVID-19 pandemic rendered face-to-face, non-socially distanced collaboration impossible and had disproportionately negative consequences on the theatre industry globally.

ProxemX comprises virtual tools that other forms of remote rehearsal, such as video conferencing, do not necessarily provide including set prototyping, 'blocking', and shared music, note taking and scripts. A major goal of the ProxemX VR environment is to support and encourage social cohesion between participants.
